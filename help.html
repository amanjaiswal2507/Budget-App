Unable to click on the edit/delete icon?

In this case just minimize the screen into smaller size. If you are using phone, you may not face this issue. In case of laptop you will face this problem. Minimize doesn't means zoom out.

Data loss & Budget problem

Note that if you reload the main budget app page, data will get lost. So, avoid that. Please enter your budget means for ex: I added initially 1000, and suppose my budget increase by 200, then you have to enter 1000+200 i.e.,1200 not 200.

Regarding edit-icon

The moment you click on the edit-icon of expense list, you will get to edit the expense title and expense amount in the expense-feedback form, where you basically added the list.
